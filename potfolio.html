Contactless Computerized Canteen Management System.

The Contactless Computerized Canteen Management System is an application developed to automate the current food inventory industry. This is possible by modern readily available software to built and maintain a database of orders and keep track of customers that dined in canteen for business and contact tracing purposes.

Problem Statement :
Inefficient use of resources like money, time and supplies at the canteen.
Overcrowding at order stations.
Errors in calculations and storage of canteen data.
Risk of exposure of diseases to canteen employees from visiting diners.
